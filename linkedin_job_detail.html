The Opportunity

We are seeking a Software Engineer to join the AEP Lakehouse team. In this position, you will contribute to constructing scalable, high-performance data systems that enable real-time customer intelligence. You will be involved in impactful projects, cooperate with experienced engineers, and get familiar with the latest data technologies in an encouraging setting. Perfect for those starting their careers wanting to advance in data and cloud expertise.

What You’ll Do

  • Write clean, efficient, and well-tested code in Java, Scala, or Python.
  • Contribute to the development of data pipelines and backend services for real-time and batch workloads.
  • Work closely with senior engineers and data scientists to assist with analytics and use cases involving artificial intelligence.
  • Participate in code reviews, testing, and release cycles to ensure high-quality software.
  • Learn and apply cloud development practices (AWS or Azure).
  • Solve issues and help improve system reliability and scalability.

What You Bring

  • Bachelor’s degree or equivalent experience in Computer Science (or related field) with 1–3 years of software engineering experience (including internships and academic projects).
  • Strong programming skills in at least one language such as Java, Python, or Scala.
  • Interest in distributed systems, cloud infrastructure, or large-scale data processing.
  • Problem-solving attitude, curiosity, and a collaborative approach to building solutions.

Nice to Have (Optional)

  • Exposure to data frameworks such as Spark, Kafka, Flink, Apache Iceberg, or Delta Lake.
  • Familiarity with containerized or cloud-native development (Kubernetes, Docker).
  • Knowledge of databases, query engines, or analytics platforms.
